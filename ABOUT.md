## 🔍 Philosophy & Design

- **Signal over Noise** — avoid overengineering, hallucinations  
- **Modular agents** — each part (scraper, inserter, API) is replaceable  
- **Self‑deploying** — with minimal manual steps  
- **Version transparency** — build metadata visible and traceable
